Biography

Hao Ren is a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University. His research focuses on embodied AI, generative models and spatial intelligence. He received his B.S. in Electronic Information Engineering from Xidian University, the M.S. degree in Computer Technology from Shaanxi University of Science & Technology. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree at Sun Yat-sen University. He has published several papers in top-tier conferences and journals such as CVPR, NeurIPS, ICRA, IEEE RA-L and EAAI. He is an active member of the academic community, serving as a reviewer for several conferences and journals.
